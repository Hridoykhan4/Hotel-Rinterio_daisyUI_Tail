FAQs about Hotel Renterio International

Find answers to some of the most commonly asked questions about your stay at Hotel Renterio. We are here to ensure a seamless experience.

What amenities are included in the rooms?

Our rooms are equipped with free Wi-Fi, air conditioning, a flat-screen TV, minibar, in-room safe, and luxurious toiletries. Guests can also request room service 24/7.

Is breakfast included in the room rate?

Yes, all bookings at Hotel Renterio International include a complimentary breakfast, served daily from 6:30 AM to 10:30 AM in our rooftop restaurant.

What is the cancellation policy?

You can cancel your reservation free of charge up to 48 hours before your scheduled check-in time. Cancellations made within 48 hours may incur a one-night stay charge.
